# Idea: <Name>

## Working definition

1–3 sentences. The English-language framing this Idea engages. State the question, not the answer. Acknowledge the framing's cultural starting point.

> *Example for Justice:* "What is owed to whom, and on what authority. The Western tradition asks this primarily as a question about persons, contracts, states, and divine law. Other traditions reframe it through cosmic order or relational propriety; see Reframings."
